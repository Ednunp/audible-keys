# Audible Keys — Privacy Policy

Audible Keys does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

The only things it saves are your own settings — your chosen keyboard shortcuts and the playback speed you set for each book. These are stored locally on your computer using Chrome's storage, and sync only through your own Chrome account if you have Chrome sync turned on. Nothing is ever sent to the developer or to any third party. There are no analytics, no tracking, and no external servers.

The extension runs only on Audible's web player pages (audible.co.uk and audible.com), where it reads the page in order to operate the player's controls (play/pause, skip, volume, speed, chapters and bookmarks). It does not read or transmit the content of your books.

Audible Keys is an independent, unofficial tool and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to Audible or Amazon.
